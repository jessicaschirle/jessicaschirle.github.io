about

I am a model theorist by training, having fallen in love with mathematical logic during my undergraduate studies for its power to describe and be applied to other areas of mathematics. I have since focused on continuous model theory as it applies to the area of geometric group theory.

I completed my Ph.D. in 2025 under the supervision of Isaac Goldbring and have since been working at the University of Minnesota, Duluth as a Teaching Assistant Professor. I am interested in refocusing my attention back to research mathematics.
